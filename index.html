About Vastra Shala

This club has been established by its lead members solely based on their passion for the art, fashion industry, styling, expressing, making statements, etc all over the world. This club aims to express one’s views, thoughts and authenticity through one’s outfits, styling, walks, etc. The club helps members and participants express their thoughts, emotions, and creativity through actions. Fashion is all about making a statement, a statement that connects with the people, a statement that shows a side of oneself to the world. It helps one express themselves authentically to the world. The club’s title “fitoor – e – kala” means “passion for art”. Which expresses how passionate we are as a team to create, showcase and execute art. Art is very abstract and subjective to each and every individual. It’s all about making one feel expressed, seen, heard, and felt. But above all, it is to be comfortable in who you are.
